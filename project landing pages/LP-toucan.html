Toucan Clothing Graphic

Client: ICHPIG

I was commissioned by ICHPIG to create a toucan graphic for an upcoming T-Shirt series they were creating.

My brief was to create a front and back graphic to be applied via screen-print to a range of different T-Shirt's using a maximum of 6 colours. After revising multiple sketch illustrations to get the basic composition and layout of the graphic, I scanned the finalised sketch and started the process of translating the sketch into a multi coloured vector graphic using Adobe Illustrator that client signed off on to be used for their t-shirt range.
