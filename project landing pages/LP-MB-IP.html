Large on the Streets Clothing Graphic

Client: ICHPIG

This is another ICHPIG illustration commmision I had the opportunity to work on. The breif was to create a 'Melbourne' graphic that showed two hands making the 'M' & 'B' with a circular crest encapsulating the graphic.

The graphic had to work in both black and white and was to be a 2 colour screenprint. After rigorous sketch develoment to create the base illustration. I worked on creating a finalised vector graphic for both the front and back of the garment that it was intended for.
